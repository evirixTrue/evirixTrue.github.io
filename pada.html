PADA

PADA stands for Philadelphia Area Disc Alliance. Founded in 1985, the organization runs ultimate frisbee leagues throughout the year for Philadelphia and it's surrounding suburban areas. Each season offers several leagues in various areas, and they range from beginner friendly to highly competitive. In addition to running these fantastic leagues, PADA hosts weekend tournaments, creates opportunities for pick-up games, drives a Youth Outreach program, and fosters a tightly knit community to grow the sport. For more information, visit pada.org. The following summer league logos appear on discs and jerseys league wide.
